who am i?

I'm Anthony Chan. I study computer science, grew up in NYC, and love to challenge myself academically. From competing in my high school math team to collaborating with others on hackathon projects, I enjoy the intellectual challenge and the process of solving a problem.
I got started in programming by creating websites for a class. From there, I explored the many facets of web development, by creating my first web application Music Mentor. Ever since, I sought to expose myself to other subfields of computer science and software development, from programming for microprocessors to building Android apps.
I am a strong believer in working hard and learning by doing. I've never stopped my journey to learn and to continue creating. I enjoy what I do.
