more about Anthony

Pianist | Runner

the full scoop:

During my free time, I like to devote a significant portion to productivity: working on projects I find interesting and learning as much as possible. Check out my GitHub to see what I've been working on!
For the remainder of my free time, I like keep a balanced lifestyle: spend time with friends and family, stay healthy (sleep, fitness, and diet), and pursue my hobbies.

i'm a pianist:

Some of my favorite pieces to play are the classical ones! I do, however, also learn to play the occasional pop song. For those who's interested, my classical repertoire currently consists of:

•Beethoven's "Fur Elise"
•Beethoven's "Moonlight Sonata (Mvt.1)"
•Yiruma's "River Flow In You"

I am currently working on several other classical pieces: Bach's "Air in G Minor," and Mozart's "Fantasy in D minor".

staying active:

I love staying active. Even during busy weeks, I try to run several times a week. I know the importance of keeping fit especially for a computer science major, because on a typical day, I would be seated for hours, either in class or in front of my laptop, doing programming.
I usually run indoors in my university's recreational center during the cold season and I run outdoors during the warm. Not only do I break a good sweat each time, but I also feel fantastic afterwards. In addition, I like to do calesthenics, weight training, and occasionally practice martial arts.
